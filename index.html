About
DAF Commons

Who We Are

We are a collective of individuals and practitioners frustrated by today's use of DAFs, and inspired by what's possible.

What We Do

The DAF Commons project was born out of this frustration, and desire to build a collective space for DAF holders seeking to make material impact with their capital. DAF Commons serves as the connective tissue between DAF holders and DAF possibilities.
